VK-MUSIC-CPP


The easiest way do download your music from vk.com to your computer!

Just choose the folder to store your music, select the music and click DOWNLOAD SELECTED

That's it!

Take a coffe and wait until download is complete.


If you need only one of your songs - just click the link and song will be downloaded.


Check if newer version is available here.

If you see any problem please don't hesitate to create an issue.

Source code is available on GitHub.


Note, that this app doesn't store or transfers your username or password from vk.com. Your credentials are only used for authentication on vk.com OAuth2.0 server.
